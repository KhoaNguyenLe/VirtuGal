A place for people to display their artwork and see what others have created


What is the goal of VirtuGal?





After the pandemic, many people lost their hobbies, their friends, and their ability to have fun showcasing their art. VirtuGal focuses on giving those people a chance to have fun again. Upload an image, enter a contest, and compete.
