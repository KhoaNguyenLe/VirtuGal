OUR GOAL

Post Pandemic, people lost their jobs, lost touch with loved one’s, and missed out on life’s
great experiences. However, art is a timeless way in which we can express ourselves despite
the circumstances and step into an immersive experience filled with wonder. VirtuGal's mission is
to cultivate a digital space for people to connect, share, and explore artistic creations.
